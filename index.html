Why SonarVision?

See what's beneath without a camera — just your depth sounder.

🎯

Zero Cameras Needed

Works with existing single-beam, multibeam, and sidescan sonar hardware. No underwater cameras, no lights, no housing.

⚡

Real-Time Inference

<50ms per frame on Jetson Orin. Sub-10ms with TensorRT. Stream live video from your survey vessel.

🧠

Self-Supervised

Trains on unlabeled depth data. No ground-truth video needed. Adapts to new water types and hardware automatically.

🌐

Federated Learning

Fleet-wide model improvement without centralizing data. Privacy-preserving, bandwidth-efficient.

🔬

Physics-Informed

Francois-Garrison absorption, Jerlov water types, Seabed models, Thermocline simulation built in.

📦

Full Stack

REST API, CLI, Docker, WebSocket streaming, Prometheus metrics, Jupyter notebooks. Ship-ready.

Use Cases

SonarVision works wherever depth sounders go.

🐟 Fisheries Research

Visualize fish schools, track DVM patterns, estimate biomass — all from standard echosounder data.

🏝️ Coastal Survey

Map benthic habitats, monitor coral reef health, detect coastal erosion without expensive camera surveys.

🚢 Maritime Security

Detect submerged objects, monitor harbor approaches, identify wrecks and debris fields in real-time.

🌋 Oceanography

Study hydrothermal vents, map underwater volcanoes, track sediment plumes and thermocline dynamics.

🧊 Polar Research

Map ice keel morphology, track under-ice topography, monitor sea-ice thickness from upward-facing sonar.

🎣 Commercial Fishing

Better catch targeting, reduced bycatch, real-time net monitoring — turn your fishfinder into underwater eyes.
